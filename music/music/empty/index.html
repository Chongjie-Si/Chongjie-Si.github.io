空无一人

Posted on 2022-05-10 In music Views: Waline: Word count in article: 2.7k Reading time ≈ 5 mins.

空无一人是我于2022年毕业季创作的歌曲。它同样也是东南大学2022年毕业季主题曲，并获得了正青春全国大学生毕业季青春新歌声人气作品优秀作品。

这首歌的词和曲是一天夜晚在校园闲逛时产生的。当时的我在体育馆的二楼边走边想，如果明天就毕业，今晚我会在做什么。我想我会在晚上和朋友们痛饮畅谈一番，结束后我会像现在这样一个人漫步在学校，回忆着四年的点点滴滴。循着这个思路，我创作了这样的一个故事：

我独自走在无人的街，时而仰望寂静的夜，路两边的单车零零散散的驶过，路灯也逐渐昏昏暗暗。我不由得感叹时光荏苒，已是离人。

我或许会想起那天傍晚，拿着情书的我犹豫不决，直到黄昏逐渐弥漫了星辰，直到我看着她默默远去，我也转身离开。如果我还在原地等待，她是否会回头？我不知道，但从此我便开始记录每一个黄昏。

我想起那天聚餐，我向朋友们许下的诺言：毕业之际必赠离别歌一曲，以纪念彼此桃花潭水深的友情。毕业之后，莫愁前路无知己，因为你我的缘分，知己海内存。

多年后也许我会想起草坪的风筝书院的清晨，会想起盛夏午时书院传来悠扬的琴声。礼堂的风默默的聆听，梧桐树毫不犹豫给了回应，而也许有一天当那些人那些事再一次出现在梦境，我再一次从睡梦中惊醒，才发现一切已是幻影，我会不会感慨不停？四年中的许多经历由于当时的自己懵懂无知没有好加珍惜，因此如今回想起便更是千思万绪百感交集。为何我会追忆此情此景？只因当时惘然的自己。

因此，我写下这首歌，纪念我的大学生活，祝福身边的同伴们，毕业快乐。

The song Empty was created by me during my graduation in June 2022. It is also the original theme song for the 2022 graduation season of Southeast University and was awarded as an excellent work in the Youth New Song Voice of the Graduation Season of the Chinese Youth League.

The lyrics and melody of this song came to me one night when I was wandering around campus. I was walking on the second floor of the gymnasium, thinking about what I would do tonight if I graduated tomorrow. I thought I would drink and chat with my friends all night, and then walk alone on campus like I am now, reminiscing about the past four years. Following this train of thought, I created this story:

I walk alone on the street, looking up at the quiet night sky from time to time. The scattered bicycles on both sides of the road pass by, and the streetlights gradually become dim. I can’t help but sigh at the fleeting time, realizing that I am about to part with my college life.

Perhaps I will remember the evening when I held a love letter, hesitant and indecisive until dusk gradually obscured the stars and I saw her quietly leaving, and I turned and left. If I had waited, would she have turned back too? I don’t know, but since then, I have started to record every dusk.

I remember the promise I made to my friends during a dinner: to give them a farewell song at graduation to commemorate our deep friendship. After graduation, there will be no worries about having no confidant on the road ahead, because our confidants are always with us.

Years later, perhaps I will remember the kite on the lawn and the melodious music from the academy in the early morning. The wind in the auditorium listens silently, the sycamore trees respond without hesitation. And perhaps one day when those people and things appear in my dreams again, I will wake up suddenly and realize that everything is just an illusion. Will I be filled with emotion? Many experiences during the four years were not cherished at that time, so now when I look back, I have many mixed feelings. Why do I recall these scenes and emotions? Only because of my melancholy self at that time.

I wrote this song to commemorate my university life and to bless my fellow graduates who are walking alongside me. Wishing us all a happy graduation!

晚霞

Posted on 2022-09-12 In music Views: Waline: Word count in article: 678 Reading time ≈ 1 mins.

从实验室的窗外望去，晚霞染红了天空，夕阳落在我的脸上。我仰望着秋日的天空，一种莫名的思乡之情从心底蔓延开来。在这种感动中，我写下了这首曲子。


        

        




When I was working in the laboratory, the gentle sunlight filtered through the window and landed on my face. As I looked out of the window, I saw the autumn evening sky filled with the glow of the sunset. I couldn’t help but feel a certain emotion in my heart, and it became tender. At the same time, a faint homesickness suddenly emerged. Therefore, with the gentle touch of the autumn sunlight and homesickness, I wrote this piece of music.
